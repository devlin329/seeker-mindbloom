Features

Classic Sudoku, Web3 Powered

All the puzzle-solving fun you love, with blockchain features that make your achievements permanent.

3 Difficulty Levels

Easy (30 blanks), Medium (40 blanks), Hard (50 blanks). Progressive challenge for all skill levels.

Timer & Scoring

Track your completion time with precision. Compete against yourself and climb the local leaderboard.

Smart Validation

Real-time validation checks rows, columns, and 3√ó3 boxes. Error highlighting helps you learn faster.

Detailed Statistics

Track total games, win rates, streaks, play time, and performance breakdown by difficulty level.

Share Your Scores

Screenshot your achievements and share on social media. Show off your puzzle-solving prowess.

Dark & Light Themes

Switch between dark and light modes. Your preference is saved and persists across sessions.

Blockchain Integration

Your Scores, Permanently On-Chain

MindBloom uses Solana's SPL Memo program to record your game completions directly on the blockchain. No centralized servers, no data loss‚Äîyour achievements are immortalized forever.

Mobile Wallet Adapter

Secure wallet connection via official Solana MWA protocol. Your keys never leave your wallet.

SPL Memo Recording

Game data stored in Solana memo transactions. Immutable, verifiable, and fee-free.

Anti-Spoofing Protection

HMAC-SHA256 signatures prevent fake score submissions. Only legitimate games count.
