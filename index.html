Game Stats Guide

Understanding Your Stats

Learn how leaderboards are ranked and what each statistic means in your gaming journey.

üèÜ Leaderboard

‚ñ∂ How Rankings Work

Leaderboards rank players by fastest completion time. Lower time = higher rank. Each difficulty level (Easy üå±, Medium ‚≠ê, Hard üî•) has its own separate leaderboard.

‚ñ∂ Personal vs Global

Personal Best: Your fastest time for each difficulty, stored locally on your device with crown emoji üëë.
Global Rankings: Blockchain-verified scores uploaded to Solana. Requires wallet connection and shows your rank among all players.

‚ñ∂ On-Chain Verification

Global leaderboard scores are recorded on Solana blockchain using SPL Memo program. Each entry includes: player nickname (max 15 chars), time, difficulty, hints used, and transaction signature for authenticity proof.

‚ñ∂ Hints & Rankings

Hints usage is tracked (0-3 per game) and displayed on leaderboard entries, but does NOT affect your ranking. Your time is what counts. Use hints freely without penalty!

üìä Statistics

‚ñ∂ Games Won vs Total Games

Games Won: Successfully completed puzzles (filled all cells correctly).
Total Games: All games started (won + abandoned/lost).
Abandoning mid-game counts as a loss and resets your streak.

‚ñ∂ Win Rate

Calculated as: (Games Won √∑ Total Games) √ó 100%
Measures your completion success rate. Higher percentage = fewer abandoned games.

‚ñ∂ Streaks üî•

Current Streak: Consecutive wins without abandoning. Tracked separately per difficulty.
Best Streak: Your highest consecutive wins record ever.
Streaks reset to 0 immediately when you abandon or lose a game.

‚ñ∂ Average Time

Total play time √∑ Games won. Only counts completed games (abandoned games excluded). Tracked per difficulty to show your improvement over time.

‚ñ∂ Hints Usage üí°

Tracks total hints used, average per game, and games completed without hints. Using hints does NOT count as a loss ‚Äî you can still maintain streaks!

üí° Pro Tips

  • ‚Ä¢ Focus on completion first: Finish puzzles to build streaks and improve win rate.
  • ‚Ä¢ Hints are free: Use them strategically without worrying about penalties or streak resets.
  • ‚Ä¢ Each difficulty is separate: Your Easy streak won't affect Medium or Hard statistics.
  • ‚Ä¢ Upload to blockchain: Connect wallet to save scores globally and compete with other players.
